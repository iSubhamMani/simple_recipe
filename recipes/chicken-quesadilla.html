Microwave Chicken Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish that consists of a tortilla filled with cheese
(and often other ingredients, such as meat and veggies).

Ingredients

  • 2 medium flour tortillas
  • ½ cup chicken strip(60 g), or shredded chicken, cooked
  • ½ bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese(25 g)

Instructions

  1. Microwave the tortillas on a plate until slightly crispy.
    They will dry out a bit as they cool.
  2. Mix the chicken, peppers, and taco seasoning in a bowl until evenly combined,
    then cook for about 1-2 minutes until the peppers are soft.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly on one tortilla, cover evenly with the cheese,
    then top with the other tortilla.
